Hi there! I'm Jose. I'm a third year computer science student, and I've been with SCS in some form since my first semester. I always enjoyed having the opportunity to meet a bunch of people from different years with distinct experiences. I've learned a lot through these people and through planning events, so I want to keep SCS standing strong for those to come. I'll be honest. The pandemic really limited that benefit, but in this last year, I hope to make the most of our return to normal! If ever you see me around, don't hesitate to say hello!

Behind the scenes, I break down our goals and delegate tasks appropriately to the team members.

VP Careers

Ariel Volovik

Email: careers.scs@ecaconcordia.ca
Major: Computer Science

VP Careers means that I'm here to try and help my fellow Uni students build a mental model of what the job searching process is like. How? By creating an ever-evolving list of things that one can prepare for, a roadmap of sorts. I'm creating workshops for one area at a time (CV/Cover Letter writing, Behavioural interview prep, Technical interview prep). The whole process requires two parties, you have to help me help you, and I shall help you help me understand what information you're missing.
I like epic music (Hans Zimmer, John Williams, if you're a Potterhead @ me). I like chatting with people so you'll often find me on Discord. I love finding general solutions to problems, be they technical or not.
